Measuring noise pollution at the World Ski Championship

This was a school project in 2025 as a part of Experts in Teamwork at NTNU.
My team consisted of me, Halvard Christoffersen, Olav Irgens Henanger, Rickard Jonassen, Morten Lindekleiv, Eryk Bartlomiej Siejka and Andreas Sollid Vanberg.

Our aim was to figure out to what extent the WSC had an effect on noise levels in the residential areas closest to the arena, and in the city centre. We measured noise levels before and during the events to establish this.

The results were surprising. We measured higher noise levels before the events than during. My guess is that this is a fluke, because of our low sample size. However, it could be that the effect comes from less motor vehicle traffic because of the events.
